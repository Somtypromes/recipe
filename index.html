Egusi Soup

Egusi is a popular melon melon seed rich in protein that is native to west african countries
it is used in its dried and grounded form as the primary ingrident to prepare the popular dish in nigeria known as egusi soup

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 1 plate
Dificulty Intermediate

Ingredients

  • palm oil: 2 cups
  • mixed spice: 1 spoonful
  • bitterleaf: 2 washed balls
  • ogiri(african spice): 2 wraps
  • water: 3 cups
  • stockfish: diced
